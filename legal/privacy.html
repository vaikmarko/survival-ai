PRIVACY PROTOCOLS

LAST UPDATED: 2025-11-24 // PROTOCOL: SEC-01

1. DATA SOVEREIGNTY

SVGN Systems operates on a principle of "Sovereign Data." Once the hardware is delivered and the "Air-Gap" switch is activated, all data generated by the system (biometrics, location, inventory) remains locally on the device. We have no backdoor access.

2. BIO-SEQUENCING DATA

During the onboarding phase, biological samples are processed by our partner laboratories. Samples are de-identified using a double-blind alphanumeric hash. Once the data is uploaded to your Sovereign OS unit, the original biological samples and digital records on our servers are securely destroyed (wiped) within 30 days.

3. NO CLOUD SYNC

By default, the Sovereign OS does not sync to any cloud service. If you choose to enable satellite uplink features (when available), you do so at your own risk regarding data interception.

4. INFORMATION COLLECTION

For the purchase process, we collect necessary KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) information as required by Estonian and International law. This includes identity verification for high-net-worth transactions.
