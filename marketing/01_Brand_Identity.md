## 3. VISUAL IDENTITY SYSTEM

### A. Color Palette
*   **Void Black:** `#050505` (Backgrounds, Hardware)
*   **Signal White:** `#F0F0F0` (Primary Text)
*   **Terminal Green:** `#00FF41` (System Status, Active Elements)
*   **Alert Amber:** `#FFB000` (Warnings, Critical Data)
*   **Slate Grey:** `#333333` (Secondary UI Elements)

### B. Typography
*   **Primary (Headlines):** `Helvetica Neue` or `Inter` (Bold, Uppercase, Tight Tracking).
    *   *Usage:* COMMANDS, HEADERS, WARNINGS.
*   **Secondary (Data/Code):** `JetBrains Mono`, `Roboto Mono`, or `Courier Prime`.
    *   *Usage:* Body text, terminal outputs, technical specs.

### C. Imagery Style
*   **Low-Light / High-Contrast:** Products shown in shadows.
*   **HUD Overlays:** Images should look like they are viewed through a drone or tactical visor.
*   **Data Visualization:** Wireframes, DNA helixes, topographical maps.
*   **No Human Faces:** The focus is on the *system* and the *user* (who is implied).

## 4. UI/UX PRINCIPLES
*   **Brutalism:** Raw, unadorned, functional.
*   **Terminal Aesthetics:** Blinking cursors, monospace loading bars, command-line interfaces.
*   **Information Density:** High. Look like a dashboard, not a brochure.
*   **Zero Latency:** Transitions should be instant or non-existent.
